Movember Mustache DJ Party & Live Pop Concert | The EndUp

Saturday, November 9, 2013 - 10:00 pm to 6:00 am | Cost: FREE*
*Free before midnight with an RSVP. Otherwise, free before 11 pm. $20 after.
The EndUp | 401 Sixth Street, San Francisco
Add to Google Calendar

Celebrate Movember: Mustache November at The EndUp with DJ Christopher B a live midnight performance by alt-popstar Colette Carr and your fabulous hosts, Khmera Rouge (Miss GAPA 2013) and Jezebel Patel (Miss Gay San Francisco 2013) on November 7, 2013.

Free before midnight with an RSVP. Otherwise, free before 11 pm. $20 after.

This is also a single release party of Paris Hilton’s new club jam “Good Time” featuring Lil Wayne. As far as we know, Paris isn’t actually going to be at the party. Come early and get in on Mustache tattoo and glasses giveaways.

ShangriLa happens on everysecond and fourth Saturday of the month at The Endup. 21+ with ID. Door opens at 10 pm.
